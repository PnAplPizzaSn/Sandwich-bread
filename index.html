Sandwich Bread

  • 3/4 c (174g) water
  • 1/2 c (125g) whole milk
  • 1 T (9g) yeast
  • 3 c (440g) bread flour
  • 1.5 t (21g) granulated sugar
  • 1 T (21g) granulated sugar
  • 3 T (42g) unsalted butter softened
    1. Combine water and milk and heat until at 98°, then mix in yeast.
    2. Cover and let sit for 10 minutes
    3. In stand mixer bowl, combine flour, salt, and sugar.
    4. Use dough hook to combine liquid with flour mixture.
    5. Add softened butter
    6. Shape dough into a ball, place dough in greased bowl and let rise at room temperature for 1 to 1 1/2 hours
    7. Lightly grease a 9 x 4 1/2 inch loaf pan
    8. Place dough on lightly floured surface. Roll out the dough until it is half an inch thick and wide enough to fit in the loaf pan.
    9. Roll dough into a log and place inside the loaf pan(seam side down), wrap and let rise at room temperature for 45 minutes to a hour
    10. Preheat oven to 350°
    11. Bake bread for 30 minutes, let cool
