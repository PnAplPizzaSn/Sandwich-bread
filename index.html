"Sandwich Bread"

  • 3/4 cup (174g) water
  • 1/2 cup (125g) whole milk
  • (9 grams)yeast
  • 3 cups (440g) bread flour
  • 1.5 teaspoons (8g) fine sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon (21g) granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter softened

Instructions

  1. Combine 3/4 c (174g) of water with 1/2 c or (125g) of milk and heat it until its around 98 degrees farhenheit, mix in about 1 tablespoon (9g) of active dry yeast
  2. cover lightly with plastic wrap and let it sit for 10 minutes
  3. Get a new bowl and combine 3 cups (440g) of bread flour, 1/2 teaspoons (8g) of fine sea salt, and 1 tablespoon(21g) granulated sugar. Whisk thorougly until combined
  4. Add flour mixture to bottom of stand mixer bowl. Attach dough hook and start mixer on low speed, slowly pour milk/water mixture. Mix until combined.
  5. Add(42g) softened butter, 1 tbsp at a time. once everything has been incorporated,knead in the machine for 30 seconds more.
  6. Take dough out of bowl and shape it into a ball, lightly grease a bowl with some vegetable or olive oil. Place dough in greased bowl and cover with greased plastic wrap. Let rise at room temperature for 1 to 1.5 hrs or until double in size.
  7. Lightly grease a 9 x 4 1/2 inch loaf pan
  8. Deflate dough and place on lightly floured surface. Using a rolling pin, roll out your dough to half an inch thick and wide enough to fit into your loaf pan.
  9. Tightly roll your dough into a log and pinch the seam. Place inside the loaf pan(seam side down), cover with greased plastic wrapand let rise at room temperature for 45 minutes to a hour or until double in size
  10. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farhenheit
  11. Place loaf pan in the oven and bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until golden brown, and then let cool.
